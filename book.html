Booking

How to secure your date.

Booking with TwoFold Visuals is straightforward. You don't need a full timeline or shot list to get started. We only need the basics to confirm your date is open and that we're a good fit.

Step one — Reach out.

Use the contact page to send your names, wedding date, contact details, ceremony and reception locations if you know them, and what you're looking for: photo, video, or both. If you don't have a venue yet, that's fine—you can send it later.

Step two — Talk through details.

We'll confirm if your date is open and share current pricing for your location and the services you want. If you'd like to talk live, we can set up a call or video chat to walk through anything that feels unclear.

Step three — Booking agreement and payment.

When you're ready, you complete the booking agreement and pay in full. Because our pricing is lower than many retainers at other companies, payment in full lets us secure travel early and keep costs down.

Timeline

Timeline planning comes later.

You don't need a finished timeline to book with us. Once the date is secured, Kelly will help you build a realistic timeline closer to the wedding day so your photo and video coverage match what you're actually planning.
