Photography

Wedding photos by Kelly Hunter.

Kelly is our partner photographer for weddings. She brings more than 20 years of experience and a calm, direct approach so you get flattering photos without feeling like you’re stuck in a photo shoot all day. She gives direction when you need it and steps back when real moments unfold, so you get both the classic portraits and the unplanned moments that actually feel like your day.

How Kelly works on a wedding day.

Kelly balances guided portraits with candid coverage. She helps build a timeline that keeps you on track without turning the day into a marathon of forced poses. Most galleries land between 300 and 500 edited photos, depending on how long the day runs and how much is happening.

  • Helps build your photo timeline before the wedding
  • Clean, true to color editing style
