Pricing

Current wedding pricing.

Our pricing adjusts based on business expenses, travel costs, and how many weddings are already booked in a given week. This page shows how we are pricing weddings today. If your location is outside our no travel fee zones, travel and lodging will be added based on actual costs.

Other locations

Pricing for weddings that need more travel.

We are happy to travel to other states. If the drive is around 4 to 5 hours or flights are needed, we add travel on top of the base price. Lodging is not included in the prices on this page. Once we know your exact locations, we calculate real travel and lodging costs and provide a clear number before you book.

Within driving distance

  • Base package price plus a travel fee when the drive is long
  • Travel fee covers fuel and time based on real distance

Flights and long distance

  • Base package price plus flights
  • Separate lodging cost based on what is needed for your location
Why it changes

Why pricing is listed as current instead of fixed.

Our prices are based on real numbers. That includes what it costs to run the business that week, how many weddings are already on the schedule, and what travel looks like. This is why you may see a slightly different number if you check back at a later date.
