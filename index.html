Booking process

How booking works.

You do not need every detail planned before you reach out.

Step two. Confirm availability.

We confirm if your date is open, share today's pricing, and answer questions. Phone or video call available if you want to talk first.

Step three. Book and plan.

Complete the booking agreement and payment. Timeline details come closer to the wedding day.
